About eboardresults.com

eboardresults.com is the official result publication portal for all Bangladesh Education Boards. It publishes exam results for millions of students sitting JSC/JDC, SSC/Dakhil, HSC/Alim and DIBS examinations — directly from the respective boards the moment results are declared.

The portal serves students checking personal grades, schools and colleges retrieving full student result lists by EIIN, district education officers accessing district-wide data, and researchers analysing board-level performance trends.

Individual / Detailed Result

The Individual Result is the most commonly used feature. Any student who has appeared in SSC, HSC, JSC or DIBS can check their personal result by entering their Roll Number and Registration Number. The result card shows subject-wise grades with colour-coded pills, the final GPA on a 5.00 scale, overall pass/fail status, and a direct link to download the official marksheet PDF from eboardresults.com.

How to Check — Step by Step

  • 1
    Select your Education Board (e.g. Dhaka, Rajshahi), the Exam Type (SSC, HSC, JSC or DIBS) and the Year of examination.
  • 2
    Enter your Roll Number (required) and Registration Number (optional but recommended — both are on your admit card).
  • 3
    A CAPTCHA image loads automatically. Type the characters shown; click ↻ Refresh if it is unclear.
  • 4
    Click "Check Individual Result". Your result card appears with subject grades, GPA, pass/fail status and a Download Marksheet PDF link.

Institution / EIIN Result

The Institution Result allows schools and colleges to retrieve the entire result list of all their students in a single query using the institution's EIIN number. The result page opens with a styled institution info card showing the school name, EIIN badge, exam centre, thana and zilla — followed by every student's grades and GPA in individual result cards. A marksheet PDF download is available for the full institution.

How to Check — Step by Step

  • 1
    Select the Education Board your institution is registered under, the Exam Type and the Year.
  • 2
    Enter your institution's unique 6-digit EIIN (Educational Institution Identification Number), issued by the Ministry of Education and found on official government documents.
  • 3
    Solve the CAPTCHA and click "Check Institution Result". The institution info card and full student result list loads.
  • 4
    Browse all student results — roll number, name, subject grades and GPA — then use the Download Marksheet PDF button to save the official document.

District Result

The District Result fetches and displays the complete exam result for an entire district in one query. Designed for district education officers and administrators who need a bird's-eye view of every institution's performance. The result is rendered in the original raw table format from eboardresults.com. A powerful Bulk PDF Downloader automatically fetches and saves marksheet PDFs for every institution in the district, with a live progress bar tracking each download.

Result Archive: 1996 to Present

One underutilized but professionally significant feature is the comprehensive result archive, which stores and makes searchable every public examination result published since 1996 — a span of over three decades.

This archive is valuable in three professional contexts: university applications abroad requiring transcript verification; government job applications under the BPSC where credentials must be independently verified; and private-sector recruitment where employers cross-reference academic credentials.

Archive Access: Historical results from 1996 through the current year are accessible through the same result-checking interface. Just select the relevant exam year from the dropdown — results are never deleted or rotated out.

How to Check Your Result — Step-by-Step

The process is straightforward, but many students make errors at key input stages. Follow these steps precisely to retrieve your result on the first attempt.

Online

Visit eboardresults.com (or use the checker above), pick exam, board & year, enter roll/registration, solve the CAPTCHA and view your full marksheet instantly.

SMS

No internet needed — text your result request to 16222 from any mobile phone and receive your GPA and pass/fail status by return SMS. Operator charges apply.

College / School Notice Board

Every institution receives the official paperless result sheet and posts it on the notice board — useful when the online server is busy on result day.

Online — Step by Step

1

Open the Official Website

Navigate to eboardresults.com in any browser. Do not use third-party mirror sites — only the official URL guarantees accurate, real-time data.

2

Select Your Examination Type

From the dropdown, choose the correct exam: SSC, HSC, Dakhil, Alim, SSC Vocational, HSC Vocational or DIBS. Selecting the wrong exam type is one of the most common errors.

3

Choose the Examination Year

Select the year the exam was held — e.g. 2026 for the current cycle. This is the academic year of the exam, not the year results are published.

4

Select Your Education Board

Pick your board (e.g. Dhaka, Comilla, Rajshahi). Madrasah or Technical students must select those specific boards, not a regional general board.

5

Choose Result Type

Select "Individual Result" for your personal result. Other options (Institution, Center, District) are aggregate views for administrators and educators.

6

Enter Roll & Registration Number

Your Roll Number is on your admit card; your Registration Number is on your registration certificate. Both are required for a full marksheet — roll alone gives a basic GPA result on some boards.

7

Solve the CAPTCHA and Submit

Enter the security code shown, then click "Get Result". The system immediately displays your full result with subject-wise grades and final GPA.

Bangladesh Grading System

Bangladesh Education Boards follow a GPA (Grade Point Average) system on a 0.00–5.00 scale for SSC, HSC, JSC and DIBS examinations. The grading scale below applies to all boards.

GradeLetter GradeMarks RangeGPA PointsStatus
A+ A Plus 80 – 100 5.00 Pass
A A Grade 70 – 79 4.00 Pass
A− A Minus 60 – 69 3.50 Pass
B B Grade 50 – 59 3.00 Pass
C C Grade 40 – 49 2.00 Pass
D D Grade 33 – 39 1.00 Pass
F F Grade 0 – 32 0.00 Fail

A student must pass all individual subjects to be declared passed overall. Failing any subject results in an overall "Fail" even if other subjects score high. Students with F grades can apply for board re-scrutiny within the declared deadline.

Final Summary

eboardresults.com is not merely a result-checking website — it is Bangladesh's centralized, authoritative digital infrastructure for public examination result publication, serving 11 education boards, multiple exam types, and a database spanning three decades.

For students it provides immediate access to individual results with full marksheets; for institutions, downloadable aggregate reports replacing paper-based processes; for administrators and researchers, statistical data that informs policy; and for professionals, a deep archive for long-term credential verification.

Knowing which exam type to select, having both roll and registration number ready, and using the SMS backup when servers are under load ensures you retrieve accurate results efficiently.
