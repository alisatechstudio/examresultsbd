Web Based Result Publication System

www.eboardresults.gov.bd is the official website managed by the Education Boards of Bangladesh for publishing public examination results, such as SSC (Secondary School Certificate), HSC (Higher Secondary Certificate), JSC (Junior School Certificate), and equivalent exams. It serves as a centralized platform where students can check their results by entering details like exam type, year, board, roll number, and registration number. The site, supported by the Ministry of Education, covers all 11 education boards in Bangladesh, including Dhaka, Chittagong, Rajshahi, and Madrasa boards, and provides access to results from 1996 onward, including detailed marksheets.

Check Result (Step-by-Step)
  1. Go to the website: www.educationboardresults.gov.bd
  2. Select your Examination (e.g. SSC, HSC, Dakhil)
  3. Choose your Year (e.g. 2026)
  4. Select your Education Board (e.g. Dhaka, Rajshahi)
  5. Enter your Roll Number
  6. Enter your Registration Number (for marksheet)
  7. Solve the security math question (e.g. 7+4)
  8. Click on the Submit button
  9. Your result will appear — you can view or print it

About the Platform

About eBoardResults.com

eBoardResults.com is Bangladesh's Web Based Result Publication System for education boards. Through a single platform, students, parents, teachers and institutions can check results for SSC, HSC, Dakhil, Alim, Vocational, JSC, JDC and DIBS examinations.

It covers all 11 education boards — nine general boards, the Bangladesh Madrasah Education Board and the Bangladesh Technical Education Board — with detailed subject-wise marksheets, board analytics and institution-level result downloads.

Results from 1996 to 2026 are available, making it the most complete educational result archive in Bangladesh. An SMS fallback to 16222 keeps results accessible even without internet.

The Education Board of Bangladesh, operating under the Ministry of Education, is the central authority responsible for overseeing and maintaining the quality of secondary and higher secondary education across the country. It manages critical functions such as curriculum development, textbook approval, and the recognition and affiliation of educational institutions to ensure standardized education.

The board conducts major public examinations, including the Secondary School Certificate (SSC), Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC), and Junior School Certificate (JSC), and is responsible for publishing accurate and transparent results through its official platform, www.educationboardresults.gov.bd.

This government web portal provides secure access to examination results, marksheet verification, and downloads, alongside institution-wise result analysis and statistical performance reports. Students can check results online by entering details like exam type, year, board, and roll number, or via SMS services. The Education Board ensures reliable services for students, institutions, and stakeholders, fostering an efficient and transparent education system in Bangladesh.
